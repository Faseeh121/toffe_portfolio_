# Frontend Features & Functionality

- **Overview**: React single-page app with React Router, Axios, context providers for auth/cart, and Helmet for SEO (frontend/src/App.js, frontend/src/index.js).
- **Navigation & Layout**: Persistent navbar with auth-aware menu, cart badge, and admin panel link; footer with quick links/support; toast notifications for feedback (frontend/src/components/Navbar.js, frontend/src/components/Footer.js, frontend/src/App.js).
- **Home Experience**: Hero CTA, promo banner, stats, category and appliance-type shortcuts, featured/top/new/smart/commercial carousels, brand logos, testimonials, newsletter block, and ad slots (frontend/src/pages/client/HomePage.js).
- **Product Discovery**: Full product listing with keyword search, category/brand/use-case/appliance-type filters, price range, smart/energy-efficient toggles, popularity filter, sort controls, and breadcrumb trail; AdSense slot on listing (frontend/src/pages/client/ProductsPage.js, frontend/src/components/Breadcrumb.js, frontend/src/components/GoogleAdsense.js).
- **Product Detail & Engagement**: Image gallery with color filtering, stock-aware quantity selection, add-to-cart / buy-now, rich description, badges, reviews list + authenticated review form, social share buttons, SEO/breadcrumb metadata, sidebar ad slot (frontend/src/pages/client/ProductDetailPage.js, frontend/src/components/ShareButtons.js, frontend/src/components/SEO.js).
- **Cart & Checkout**: LocalStorage-backed cart with quantity edits and removal, order summary (subtotal/shipping/tax), guarded checkout flow, shipping form, cash-on-delivery payment option, and order creation with success screen (frontend/src/pages/client/CartPage.js, frontend/src/pages/client/CheckoutPage.js, frontend/src/pages/client/OrderSuccessPage.js).
- **Accounts & Auth**: Email/password login and registration with localStorage persistence, protected routes via `PrivateRoute`, profile management (personal info, address, optional password change) (frontend/src/context/AuthContext.js, frontend/src/components/PrivateRoute.js, frontend/src/pages/client/LoginPage.js, frontend/src/pages/client/RegisterPage.js, frontend/src/pages/client/ProfilePage.js).
- **Orders**: Authenticated order history list with status badges and totals plus detailed order view (items, shipping, payment/delivery status); inline order ID view after checkout (frontend/src/pages/client/MyOrdersPage.js, frontend/src/pages/client/OrderDetailPage.js).
- **Growth & SEO**: Reusable SEO component injects meta tags, OpenGraph/Twitter, canonical links, product schema, and breadcrumbs; Google Analytics with custom pageview logging to backend; share buttons and newsletter capture (frontend/src/components/SEO.js, frontend/src/components/GoogleAnalytics.js, frontend/src/components/ShareButtons.js, frontend/src/components/NewsletterSignup.js).
- **Monetization & Config**: AdSense helper with site-settings toggles for slot enablement; ads placed across home, listing, product detail, and footer; settings hook fetches SEO/adsense config from backend (frontend/src/components/GoogleAdsense.js, frontend/src/hooks/useSiteSettings.js).
- **Performance/UX touches**: Loading guards for auth and data fetches, responsive navbar/menu, badges for discounts/smart/eco products, and persisted cart totals (frontend/src/components/PrivateRoute.js, frontend/src/context/CartContext.js, frontend/src.pages/client/ProductsPage.js).

## Demo Flow (talk track)
1) Land on Home to showcase hero, promo, categories, featured and smart sections, and newsletter/ads. 2) Jump to Products to apply filters (e.g., smart + energy efficient) and sorting. 3) Open a product detail, swap colors/images, add to cart, and submit a review (after login). 4) View cart, proceed to checkout, fill shipping, place COD order, and show success page. 5) Visit My Orders and drill into an order. 6) Visit Profile to edit info/password. 7) Point out SEO tags, analytics, and ad placements running in the background.
